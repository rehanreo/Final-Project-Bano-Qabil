A Legacy Born Before Pakistan

In 1945 — two years before Pakistan's independence — Muhammad Saleh began making and selling rope in Karachi. His honest dealing and quality work built a reputation that spread by word of mouth.

His son, M. Hanif Saleh, carried the business forward with the same values. Today, the third generation continues the legacy — serving fishermen, farmers, builders, and households across Pakistan.
